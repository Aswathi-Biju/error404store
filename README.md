# 🛒 Error 404 Store

### *Products Not Found*

> “Buy what doesn’t exist.”

---

## 🧠 Overview

**Error 404 Store** is a deceptive e-commerce web experience designed for the **Prankraft – Web Design Competition**.

At first glance, it appears to be a premium online store offering curated lifestyle and productivity products. However, as users interact with the platform, they gradually realize that the products are **absurd, ironic, and ultimately non-existent**.

The project explores the idea that:

> *Design is not just how it looks — but how it makes you feel, think, and react.*

---

## 🎯 Concept

This project is built around the theme:

### ✨ *“Design Deceives”*

The goal is to:

* Create a **realistic and believable interface**
* Introduce **subtle inconsistencies**
* Deliver a **clever and memorable prank reveal**

---

## 🛍️ Features

* 🖥️ Clean and modern e-commerce UI
* 🛒 Product listing with humorous, ironic items
* 📄 Individual product details
* 🧾 Cart and checkout flow
* 🎭 Final prank reveal experience
* 🌑 Minimalist dark-themed design

---

## 🎨 Design Approach

* **Minimal & Premium UI**
  Inspired by modern product websites

* **Subtle Humor**
  Calm, serious tone describing ridiculous products

* **User Experience First**
  Designed to guide users through a journey:

  1. Trust
  2. Curiosity
  3. Realization
  4. Surprise
